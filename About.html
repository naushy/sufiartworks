Date of Birth: 4th august, 1942
Birth: Jodhpur, Rajasthan

With a touch of mysticism , Artist Ishaque Ahmed has dedicated his life towards making contribution in philanthropy through the artwork with impeccable creativity. While choosing warm colors as a token of native place, he has created such expressions in Paintings that have left many with wonder. Genius in matter of subtle color spectrums & display combinational virtuosity in Photography & Paintings as the merit.

Earlier Work:

  • 1961-1962, Indore, Textile Industry (Hukmi Chandra mill), worked as Textile Designer
  • 1963, Jodhpur, Teacher in Ishaqiya school
  • 1965- till date of retirement, Jodhpur, Worked in Education Dept., got selected & moved to J.N.V.U.

    • 1967-2002, Career, Dextrous Artist cum Photographer in J.N.V.U., Zoology Department, Assistance to Phd. scholars
    • After 1980, contributions in Artist Gaddi Workshop in Jaipur, Rajasthan Lalit-Kala Academy, Pallete and various other prestigious exhibitions/art-camps being organized in Rajasthan & Delhi.

Domain

Painting(Pastel, Acrylic, Oil), Serigraphy, Calligraphy, Photography(micro, macro), Philosophy, Sufi writing etc.
